Our goals

We want to raise privacy awareness among the general population. Today, most people seem to have an unconditional faith in the every free service encountered, with the exception of security industry professionals. We want to educate people about the risks of sharing personal data in the Internet, and how to enjoy the benefits of social media while minimizing the risk of exploitation. We want to provide:

  • Resources people can look to for help on how to avoid being exploited.
  • Sharing guidelines for social media.
  • Technical guidelines for companies wanting the benefits of big data without abusing users trust.
